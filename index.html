Our Story

Crave Corner - 22 Years of Culinary Excellence

Founded 22 years ago, Crave Corner has been a beloved destination for food lovers seeking exceptional flavors. Under the guidance of our renowned chef, Vineet Bhatia, we have crafted a menu that combines innovation with tradition, offering dishes that satisfy every craving. With decades of experience, Crave Corner has grown from a small local spot to a must-visit for anyone seeking a memorable dining experience. Join us as we continue to serve culinary delights with passion and precision!

Meet Our Chef

Mr. Vineet Bhatia

Mr. Bhatia was recruited as a trainee to the Oberoi School of Hotel Management, New Delhi in 1988 where he trained until 1990. He went on to work as Chef de Cuisine at The Oberoi from 1990 to 1993. He moved to London in 1993, taking on the position of Executive Chef at Star of India, South Kensington.

Mr. Bhatia has written two cookbooks:

Rasoi : New Indian Kitchen, 2009

My Sweet Kitchen, 2016
